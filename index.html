Welcome to SikeNike

From 1 to 4 hiders take turns hiding while the bot seeker hunts them down.

Each hider gets 30 seconds to choose a spot. Once locked, they cannot move again.

There are 8 levels: basement 2, basement 1, ground, level 1, level 2, level 3, level 4, and roof. Right stairs go up, left stairs go down.

Each floor has a K tile. Touch K to change that floor's secret door from locked S to open O for the current turn.

The seeker then has 3 minutes to find everyone.
